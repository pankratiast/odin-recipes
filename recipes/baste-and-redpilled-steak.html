Baste and Redpilled Steak

Description

A flavorful and perfectly cooked steak, seasoned simply and basted with butter, garlic, and rosemary for a rich, savory experience. This method ensures a juicy, tender cut, ideal for steak lovers looking for a classic preparation. You can also watch this 2:30 minute video from Gordon Ramsay which is great here for a classic preparation.

Ingredients

  • Steak (ribeye or New York strip, your choice)
  • Salt (or garlic salt, if preferred)
  • Butter (salted, if preferred)
  • Minced garlic (or fresh garlic, finely chopped)
  • 1 clove rosemary
  • Pepper as a finish

Steps

  1. Remove the steak from the fridge and let it sit out until it reaches room temperature.
  2. Season both sides of the steak with salt (or garlic salt, if preferred).
  3. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat with butter and minced garlic until melted and fragrant.
  4. Place the steak in the skillet and cook on one side, rendering fat on the sides if necessary.
  5. Flip the steak, add a clove of rosemary to the skillet, and use a spoon to repeatedly scoop the fat, garlic, and butter mixture over the steak.
  6. Remove the steak from the skillet when done to your liking and place it on a plate.
  7. Add pepper on top to taste
