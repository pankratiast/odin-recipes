Shakshuka

Description

A simple and delicious shakshuka featuring eggs cooked over easy/medium in a skillet with sweet cherry tomatoes and a dollop of low-fat cottage cheese. Enhanced with optional pepper and red pepper flakes, this dish is perfect for dipping bread to scoop up the flavorful egg, cheese, and tomato mixture.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Sweet cherry tomatoes (about 1 cup)
  • Low-fat cottage cheese (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste, optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (to taste, optional)
  • Bread (for dipping)

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over low heat. Add the sweet cherry tomatoes and mash them gently with a spoon or spatula until they release their juices and form a saucy base, cooking for about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Create small wells in the tomato mixture and crack the 3 eggs into the wells. Cook the eggs over medium heat until they reach an over easy/medium consistency (about 3-5 minutes), covering the skillet to help the eggs set.
  3. Dollop low-fat cottage cheese into the skillet, distributing it around the eggs.
  4. Season with pepper and red pepper flakes to taste, if desired.
  5. Serve hot, dipping bread into the skillet to scoop up the egg, cheese, and tomato mixture.
