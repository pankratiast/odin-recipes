Chicken Fried Rice

Description

A simple and quick chicken fried rice made with minimal ingredients. This dish combines tender cubed chicken with flavorful Kroger brand frozen fried rice, finished with scrambled eggs for a hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken cut and cubed (about 2-3 lbs)
  • Kroger brand frozen fried rice
  • 2-3 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a skillet, cook the Kroger brand frozen fried rice over medium heat, frying it to your desired texture.
  2. Push the fried rice to one side of the skillet. Crack 2-3 eggs into the empty side, stir until fully cooked, then mix the eggs into the rice.
  3. In a separate skillet, cook the cubed chicken with salt and pepper until fully cooked and golden brown.
  4. Serve the chicken alongside or mixed into the fried rice, and enjoy!
